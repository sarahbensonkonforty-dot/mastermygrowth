About Sarah

Someone who understands
the whole person

Dr. Sarah Benson-Konforty is a serial entrepreneur, investor, and life transition coach based in Palo Alto, CA. She founded her first software company at 18, earned her MD, studied entrepreneurship and innovation at Wharton, and built a career as a globally recognized founder, board member, and executive across multiple industries.

But behind the credentials is a fuller story. Sarah has navigated marriage and divorce, relocated three times across three different continents, raised two wonderful teenagers largely on her own, and continued building companies through all of it. She knows what it means to rebuild your life — your identity, your community, your sense of home — while still showing up for everyone else.

That lived experience is what she brings to her coaching practice. My role isn't to tell you what to do. It's to help you see more clearly, uncover what's holding you back, and move forward with greater clarity, courage, and purpose.
